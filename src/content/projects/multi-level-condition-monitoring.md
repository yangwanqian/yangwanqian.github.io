This project studies decision-support-oriented multi-level intelligent condition monitoring of wind turbines.

The research connects turbine-level operating state representation, subsystem-level heterogeneous monitoring, gearbox degradation monitoring, blade crack propagation prediction, and predictive maintenance strategy optimization.
